## Confirm Delete 

Always add an additional confirmation step before allowing the user to delete data.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Confirm Delete">
    <Button small theme="danger" onClick={() => confirm('Are you sure?')}>
      Delete
    </Button>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Saving a Form

Do not combine icons and words together in buttons, unless it is to represent 
some sort of active state such as saving in progress.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Saving a Form">
    <Button theme="success" disabled>
      <Icon name="spinner" spin /> Saving...
    </Button>
  </Story>
</Canvas>
